## üéÆ **Concept Overview**

Transform Turbo Shells from manual race watching to an automated racing management system where players observe ambient races while managing other aspects of their turtle empire. This creates a true management simulation with continuous gameplay flow.

---

## üèÅ **Automated Race System**

### **Core Philosophy**
- **Background Racing**: Races run automatically while player manages other activities
- **Ambient Observation**: Players can watch races progress while managing breeding, training, and glade activities
- **Progress Monitoring**: Gentle progress indicators hover during races
- **Multi-tasking**: Seamless switching between activities without interrupting races

### **Race Progress Display**
- **Floating Progress Bar**: Small, elegant progress indicator that hovers on screen
- **Minimalist Design**: Non-intrusive, semi-transparent overlay
- **Race Status**: Shows current race progress, time elapsed, estimated completion
- **Quick Access**: Click to jump to full race view if desired

### **Background Race Mechanics**
- **Continuous Racing**: Queue multiple races for automated execution
- **Speed Control**: Adjustable race speeds (1x, 2x, 4x, 8x, 16x)
- **Auto-Selection**: Intelligent turtle selection based on terrain and opponents
- **Batch Racing**: Run multiple races sequentially while player manages other tasks

## üîÆ **Vision Statement**

The Auto-Racing System transforms Turbo Shells into a living turtle management simulation where players nurture generations of turtles through automated racing, natural breeding, and ecosystem management. Players become true turtle masters, observing their legacy grow through peaceful pond environments while managing a thriving turtle empire.

This system creates endless gameplay variety through procedural generation, genetic diversity, and dynamic ecosystems, while maintaining the core appeal of turtle racing as the foundation of the player's economic and strategic decisions.
